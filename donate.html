Altadena Fire Relief Fund

Powered by EQNOIR

Altadena’s predominantly Black residents have been disproportionately impacted by recent wildfires — and largely ignored by traditional aid channels.

That’s why EQNOIR is stepping up with a 100 billion token commitment dedicated solely to Altadena relief, rebuilding, and long-term support.

We are currently identifying local partners and trusted community organizations in Altadena.

If you or your group would like to partner with us, please email: eqnoirproject@gmail.com

Let’s show what decentralized power can do when it’s focused on real people, in real need.
